How to Use View Market

About View Market

Welcome to our online marketplace for vegetables, metals, meat, and fuels. Our platform provides real-time price updates and connects buyers and sellers, making it easy to find and trade quality products.

Getting Started

  1. Sign Up: Create an account by providing basic details.
  2. Login: Use your credentials to access your dashboard.
  3. Check Prices: View real-time prices of vegetables, metals, meat, and fuels.
  4. Browse Listings: Explore available products in different categories.

Real-Time Price Updates

Our website provides the latest market prices for:

  • Vegetables: Prices fluctuate based on supply, demand, and season.
  • Metals: Check daily updates for gold,silver and platinum .
